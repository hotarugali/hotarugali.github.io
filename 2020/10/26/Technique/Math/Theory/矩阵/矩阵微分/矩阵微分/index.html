独立性假设

假定实值函数的向量变元 x=[xi]i=1m∈Rm \boldsymbol{x} = [x_i]_{i=1}^m \in \mathbb{R}^m x=[xi​]i=1m​∈Rm 或者矩阵变元 X=[xij]i=1,j=1m,n∈Rm×n \boldsymbol{X} = [x_{ij}]_{i=1,j=1}^{m,n} \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n} X=[xij​]i=1,j=1m,n​∈Rm×n 本身无任何特殊结构，即向量或矩阵变元的元素之间是各自独立的。用数学公式表达如下：

∂xi∂xj=δij={1i=j0others∂xkl∂xij=δkiδlj={1k=i∩l=j0others\begin{array}{c} \frac{\partial x_i}{\partial x_j} = \delta_{ij} = \begin{cases} 1 & i = j \\ 0 & others \end{cases} \\ \frac{\partial x_{kl}}{\partial x_{ij}} = \delta_{ki} \delta_{lj} = \begin{cases} 1 & k = i \cap l = j \\ 0 & others \end{cases} \end{array} ∂xj​∂xi​​=δij​={10​i=jothers​∂xij​∂xkl​​=δki​δlj​={10​k=i∩l=jothers​​

1. 符号定义及实值函数分类

2. 定义

2.1 矩阵的向量化

  • 列向量化：矩阵 A∈Rm×n\boldsymbol{A} \in \mathbb{R}^{m \times n}A∈Rm×n 的向量化 vec(A)\mathrm{vec}(\boldsymbol{A})vec(A) 是一个线性变换，它将矩阵 A=[aij] \boldsymbol{A} = [a_{ij}] A=[aij​] 的元素按列堆栈，排列成一个 mn×1mn \times 1mn×1 的向量，即

vec(A)=[a11,⋯ ,am1,⋯ ,a1n,⋯ ,amn]T\begin{array}{c} \mathrm{vec}(\boldsymbol{A}) = [a_{11}, \cdots, a_{m1}, \cdots, a_{1n}, \cdots, a_{mn}]^T \end{array} vec(A)=[a11​,⋯,am1​,⋯,a1n​,⋯,amn​]T​

  • 行向量化：类似地，矩阵 A\boldsymbol{A}A 的行向量化为

rvec(A)=[a11,⋯ ,a1n,⋯ ,am1,⋯ ,amn]\begin{array}{c} \mathrm{rvec}(\boldsymbol{A}) = [a_{11}, \cdots, a_{1n}, \cdots, a_{m1}, \cdots, a_{mn}] \end{array} rvec(A)=[a11​,⋯,a1n​,⋯,am1​,⋯,amn​]​

2.2 向量的矩阵化

一个 mn×1mn \times 1mn×1 向量 a=[a1,⋯ ,amn]T \boldsymbol{a} = [a_1, \cdots, a_{mn}]^T a=[a1​,⋯,amn​]T 转换为一个 m×nm \times nm×n 矩阵 A\boldsymbol{A}A 的运算称为矩阵化，用符号 unvecm,n(a)\mathrm{unvec}_{m,n}(\boldsymbol{a})unvecm,n​(a) 表示，定义为

Am×n=unvecm,n(a)=[a1am+1⋯am(n−1)+1a2am+2⋯am(n−1)+2⋮⋮⋱⋮ama2m⋯amn]\begin{array}{c} \boldsymbol{A}_{m \times n} = \mathrm{unvec}_{m, n}(\boldsymbol{a}) = \left[ \begin{matrix} a_1 & a_{m+1} & \cdots & a_{m(n-1)+1} \\ a_2 & a_{m+2} & \cdots & a_{m(n-1)+2} \\ \vdots & \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ a_m & a_{2m} & \cdots & a_{mn} \end{matrix} \right] \end{array} Am×n​=unvecm,n​(a)=⎣⎡​a1​a2​⋮am​​am+1​am+2​⋮a2m​​⋯⋯⋱⋯​am(n−1)+1​am(n−1)+2​⋮amn​​⎦⎤​​

2.3 偏导算子

  • 1×m1 \times m1×m 行向量偏导算子记为：

Dx=def∂∂xT=[∂∂x1,⋯ ,∂∂xm]\begin{array}{c} D_{\boldsymbol{x}} \overset{\mathrm{def}}{=} \frac{\partial}{\partial \boldsymbol{x}^T} = [ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_1}, \cdots, \frac{\partial}{\partial x_m} ] \end{array} Dx​=def∂xT∂​=[∂x1​∂​,⋯,∂xm​∂​]​

  • n×mn \times mn×m 矩阵偏导算子存在两种可能的定义，分别记为：

DX=[∂∂x11⋯∂∂xm1⋮⋱⋮∂∂x1n⋯∂∂xmn]\begin{array}{c} D_{\boldsymbol{X}} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{11}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{m1}} \\ \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{1n}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{mn}} \end{matrix} \right] \end{array} DX​=⎣⎡​∂x11​∂​⋮∂x1n​∂​​⋯⋱⋯​∂xm1​∂​⋮∂xmn​∂​​⎦⎤​​

和

DrvecX=[∂∂x11,⋯ ,∂∂xm1,⋯ ,∂∂x1n,⋯ ,∂∂xmn]\begin{array}{c} D_{\mathrm{rvec} \boldsymbol{X}} = [\frac{\partial}{\partial x_{11}}, \cdots, \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{m1}}, \cdots, \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{1n}}, \cdots, \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{mn}}] \end{array} DrvecX​=[∂x11​∂​,⋯,∂xm1​∂​,⋯,∂x1n​∂​,⋯,∂xmn​∂​]​

前者称为矩阵变元 X\boldsymbol{X}X 的 Jacobian 矩阵算子，后者称为矩阵变元 X\boldsymbol{X}X 列向量化（vec\mathrm{vec}vec）后的行偏导向量算子。此二者关系为：

DrvecX=rvec(DX)=(vec(DXT))T\begin{array}{c} D_{\mathrm{rvec}\boldsymbol{X}} = \mathrm{rvec}(D_{\boldsymbol{X}}) = (\mathrm{vec}(D_{\boldsymbol{X}}^T))^T \end{array} DrvecX​=rvec(DX​)=(vec(DXT​))T​

  • m×1m \times 1m×1 列向量偏导算子（习惯称为梯度算子）记为：

∇x=def∂∂x=[∂∂x1,⋯ ,∂∂xm]T\begin{array}{c} \nabla_{\boldsymbol{x}} \overset{\mathrm{def}}{=} \frac{\partial}{\partial \boldsymbol{x}} = [\frac{\partial}{\partial x_1}, \cdots, \frac{\partial}{\partial x_m}]^T \end{array} ∇x​=def∂x∂​=[∂x1​∂​,⋯,∂xm​∂​]T​

  • n×mn \times mn×m 矩阵梯度算子存在两种可能的定义，分别记为：

∇X=[∂∂x11⋯∂∂x1n⋮⋱⋮∂∂xm1⋯∂∂xmn]\begin{array}{c} \nabla_{\boldsymbol{X}} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{11}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{1n}} \\ \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{m1}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{mn}} \end{matrix} \right] \end{array} ∇X​=⎣⎡​∂x11​∂​⋮∂xm1​∂​​⋯⋱⋯​∂x1n​∂​⋮∂xmn​∂​​⎦⎤​​

和

∇vecX=∂∂vecX=[∂∂x11,⋯ ,∂∂xm1,⋯ ,∂∂x1n,⋯ ,∂∂xmn]T\begin{array}{c} \nabla_{\mathrm{vec}\boldsymbol{X}} = \frac{\partial}{\partial_{\mathrm{vec}\boldsymbol{X}}} = [\frac{\partial}{\partial x_{11}}, \cdots, \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{m1}}, \cdots, \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{1n}}, \cdots, \frac{\partial}{\partial x_{mn}}]^T \end{array} ∇vecX​=∂vecX​∂​=[∂x11​∂​,⋯,∂xm1​∂​,⋯,∂x1n​∂​,⋯,∂xmn​∂​]T​

前者称为矩阵变元 X\boldsymbol{X}X 的梯度矩阵算子，后者称为梯度向量算子。此二者关系为：

∇vecX=vec(∇X)\begin{array}{c} \nabla_{\mathrm{vec} \boldsymbol{X}} = \mathrm{vec}(\nabla_{\boldsymbol{X}}) \end{array} ∇vecX​=vec(∇X​)​

由以上定义可知，行向量算子和列向量算子、Jacobian 矩阵算子和梯度矩阵算子、行偏导向量算子和梯度向量算子之间的关系为：

∇x=DxT∇X=DXT∇vecX=DvecXT\begin{array}{c} \nabla_{\boldsymbol{x}} = D_{\boldsymbol{x}}^T \\ \nabla_{\boldsymbol{X}} = D_{\boldsymbol{X}}^T \\ \nabla_{\mathrm{vec}\boldsymbol{X}} = D_{\mathrm{vec}\boldsymbol{X}}^T \end{array} ∇x​=DxT​∇X​=DXT​∇vecX​=DvecXT​​

可以看到，梯度向量等于行偏导向量的转置。在此意义上，行向量偏导向量是列向量形式的梯度向量的协变形式，故又称行偏导向量为协梯度向量。类似地，Jacobian 矩阵也称为协梯度矩阵。

∇Xf(X)=∂f(X)∂X=[∂f(X)∂x11⋯∂f(X)∂x1n⋮⋱⋮∂f(X)∂xm1⋯∂f(X)∂xmn]\begin{array}{c} \nabla_{\boldsymbol{X}} f(\boldsymbol{X}) = \frac{\partial f(\boldsymbol{X})}{\partial \boldsymbol{X}} = \left[ \begin{matrix} \frac{\partial f(\boldsymbol{X})}{\partial x_{11}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial f(\boldsymbol{X})}{\partial x_{1n}} \\ \vdots & \ddots & \vdots \\ \frac{\partial f(\boldsymbol{X})}{\partial x_{m1}} & \cdots & \frac{\partial f(\boldsymbol{X})}{\partial x_{mn}} \end{matrix} \right] \end{array} ∇X​f(X)=∂X∂f(X)​=⎣⎡​∂x11​∂f(X)​⋮∂xm1​∂f(X)​​⋯⋱⋯​∂x1n​∂f(X)​⋮∂xmn​∂f(X)​​⎦⎤​​

3.2 常用性质

  • 对于实值标量函数 f(x)=xTAx f(\boldsymbol{x}) = \boldsymbol{x}^T \boldsymbol{A} \boldsymbol{x} f(x)=xTAx，其中 A∈Rn×n,x∈Rn×1\boldsymbol{A} \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times n}, \boldsymbol{x} \in \mathbb{R}^{n \times 1}A∈Rn×n,x∈Rn×1，其行偏导向量和矩阵向量为
